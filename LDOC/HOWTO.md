## How to start LDOC

- use path of config.ld 
- relative path back to LDOC to use: ./lua5.4 ldoc.lua .

## Example
- ./../../../../tools/LDOC/LDoc-1.4.6/lua5.4 ../../../../tools/LDOC/LDoc-1.4.6/ldoc.lua .
